# Light Novel Vocabulary

## School Terms
先輩 (senpai) - senior student, upperclassman
後輩 (kouhai) - junior student, underclassman
部活 (bukatsu) - club activities
生徒会 (seitokai) - student council
文化祭 (bunkasai) - cultural festival
体育祭 (taiikusai) - sports festival
教室 (kyoushitsu) - classroom
廊下 (rouka) - hallway, corridor
屋上 (okujou) - rooftop
保健室 (hokenshitsu) - infirmary, nurse's office
図書館 (toshokan) - library
校舎 (kousha) - school building

## Character Types & Relationships  
幼馴染 (osananajimi) - childhood friend
親友 (shinyuu) - best friend
ライバル (raibaru) - rival
委員長 (iinchou) - committee chairperson, class president
転校生 (tenkousei) - transfer student
天才 (tensai) - genius
ツンデレ (tsundere) - tsundere personality type
クーデレ (kuudere) - kuudere personality type

## Emotions & Expressions
恥ずかしい (hazukashii) - embarrassed, shy
ドキドキ (dokidoki) - heart beating fast, excitement
照れる (tereru) - to be shy, embarrassed
赤面 (sekimen) - blushing, red face
緊張 (kinchou) - nervous, tension
感動 (kandou) - being moved, touched emotionally
憧れ (akogare) - admiration, longing
嫉妬 (shitto) - jealousy

## Romance & Relationships
告白 (kokuhaku) - confession of love
恋人 (koibito) - lover, boyfriend/girlfriend  
片思い (kataomoi) - unrequited love
両想い (ryouomoi) - mutual love
デート (deeto) - date
プレゼント (purezento) - present, gift
手紙 (tegami) - letter
思い出 (omoide) - memories

## Common Light Novel Phrases
頑張って (ganbatte) - do your best, good luck
お疲れ様 (otsukaresama) - good work, thanks for your effort
いらっしゃいませ (irasshaimase) - welcome (formal greeting)
ただいま (tadaima) - I'm home
お帰りなさい (okaerinasai) - welcome home
いってきます (ittekimasu) - I'm leaving/going
いってらっしゃい (itterasshai) - have a safe trip
