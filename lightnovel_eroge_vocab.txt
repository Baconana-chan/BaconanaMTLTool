# Erotic Light Novel Vocabulary

## Physical & Emotional Terms
愛撫 (aibu) - caress, intimate touch
吐息 (toiki) - sigh, breath, panting
鼓動 (kodou) - heartbeat, pulse
体温 (taion) - body temperature, warmth
肌 (hada) - skin
唇 (kuchibiru) - lips
瞳 (hitomi) - eyes, pupils
髪 (kami) - hair
香り (kaori) - scent, fragrance
温もり (nukumori) - warmth

## Intimate Expressions
感じる (kanjiru) - to feel (sensation)
触れる (fureru) - to touch, brush against
抱く (daku) - to embrace, hold
寄り添う (yorisou) - to snuggle up to, nestle
もたれる (motareru) - to lean against
震える (furueru) - to tremble, shiver
火照る (hoteru) - to flush, burn with heat
切ない (setsunai) - painful longing, bittersweet

## Romantic Atmosphere
ムード (muudo) - mood, atmosphere
雰囲気 (fun'iki) - atmosphere, ambiance
ロマンチック (romanchikku) - romantic
甘い (amai) - sweet (atmosphere)
官能的 (kannou-teki) - sensual
情熱的 (jounetsu-teki) - passionate
優しい (yasashii) - gentle, tender
激しい (hageshii) - intense, passionate

## Emotional States
恥じらい (hajirai) - bashfulness, modesty
ときめき (tokimeki) - heart flutter, excitement
胸の高鳴り (mune no takanari) - heart racing
切望 (setsubou) - longing, yearning
欲望 (yokubou) - desire
衝動 (shoudou) - impulse, urge

## Body Language & Actions
頬を染める (hoho wo someru) - cheeks flushing
瞳を潤ませる (hitomi wo urumaseru) - eyes moistening
息を荒げる (iki wo arageru) - breathing heavily
身を委ねる (mi wo yudaneru) - to entrust oneself to
寄り添う (yorisou) - to cuddle up, nestle close
見つめ合う (mitsumeau) - to gaze into each other's eyes

## Intimate Settings
二人きり (futari kiri) - just the two of them, alone together
密室 (misshitsu) - closed room, private space
ベッドルーム (beddoruumu) - bedroom
バスルーム (basuruumu) - bathroom
夜景 (yakei) - night view
月明かり (tsukiakari) - moonlight
キャンドルライト (kyandoru raito) - candlelight
